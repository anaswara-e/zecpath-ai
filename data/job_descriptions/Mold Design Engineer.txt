Job Summary
A Mold Design Engineer specializes in designing molds used in injection molding, blow
molding, and other plastic manufacturing processes.
Key Responsibilities
• Design plastic injection molds and tooling systems
• Create detailed CAD models and mold flow analysis
• Select materials and components for mold construction
• Optimize mold design for cycle time and quality
• Collaborate with production and tooling teams
• Troubleshoot mold-related issues and defects
• Ensure compliance with industry standards
Required Skills
• Expertise in mold design software (NX, SolidWorks, Moldflow)
• Knowledge of plastics and injection molding processes
• Understanding of cooling systems and gating design
• Attention to detail
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical/Tool Engineering
• 3–8 years experience
