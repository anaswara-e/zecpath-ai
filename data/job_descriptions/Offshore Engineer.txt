Job Summary
An Offshore Engineer works on oil & gas platforms and offshore installations, focusing on
equipment, structures, and systems used in harsh marine environments.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and maintain offshore structures and equipment
• Support installation and commissioning of offshore systems
• Monitor equipment performance and ensure reliability
• Conduct inspections and maintenance in offshore environments
• Ensure compliance with safety and environmental regulations
• Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams
• Manage risks associated with offshore operations

• Prepare reports and technical documentation
Required Skills
• Knowledge of offshore engineering and marine systems
• Understanding of oil & gas processes
• Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
• Ability to work in offshore conditions (rotational shifts)
• Safety and risk management expertise
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• Offshore certifications (BOSIET, HUET – preferred)
• 3–7 years of experience
