Job Summary
An Assembly Line Engineer is responsible for designing, managing, and optimizing
assembly line operations to ensure smooth production flow, efficiency, and product quality.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and optimize assembly line layouts
• Balance workloads across assembly stations
• Monitor assembly line performance and efficiency
• Identify and resolve bottlenecks in production flow
• Implement automation and process improvements
• Ensure quality standards are maintained during assembly
• Train operators and supervise assembly processes
• Maintain documentation and performance reports
Required Skills
• Knowledge of assembly processes and line balancing
• Understanding of Lean Manufacturing principles
• Problem-solving and operational management skills
• Familiarity with automation systems (preferred)
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–7 years experience
