Job Summary
An Automotive Engineer works on the design, development, testing, and manufacturing of
vehicles and their subsystems, ensuring performance, safety, and compliance with industry
standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and develop automotive components and systems
• Conduct testing and validation of vehicle performance
• Collaborate with design, production, and quality teams
• Ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards
• Analyze performance data and improve vehicle efficiency
• Support prototype development and testing
• Troubleshoot design and manufacturing issues
Required Skills
• Knowledge of automotive systems (engine, transmission, chassis)
• CAD tools (CATIA, Creo, NX)
• Understanding of vehicle dynamics
• Problem-solving and analytical skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Automotive Engineering
• 2–6 years of experience
