Job Summary
The Head of Quality is a strategic leadership role responsible for defining and driving the
organization’s overall quality vision, ensuring excellence across all operations, and aligning
quality strategies with business goals.
Key Responsibilities
• Define and implement organization-wide quality strategy
• Oversee all quality functions (QA, QC, supplier quality, audits)
• Ensure compliance with global standards and regulatory requirements
• Drive a culture of quality and continuous improvement
• Lead large quality teams across multiple locations or plants
• Establish and monitor key quality KPIs and performance metrics
• Manage customer quality relationships and escalations
• Drive digital transformation in quality systems (QMS, analytics)
• Collaborate with executive leadership on business strategy
• Lead certification processes and external audits
Required Skills
• Strong strategic leadership and decision-making
• Deep expertise in quality systems and compliance
• Advanced knowledge of Lean, Six Sigma, and operational excellence
• Stakeholder and customer management
• Data analytics and performance management
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (MBA preferred)
• 12–18+ years of experience
• Six Sigma Black Belt / Master Black Belt (preferred)
