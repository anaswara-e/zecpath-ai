Job Summary
A Supply Chain Manager is responsible for managing procurement, logistics, inventory, and
distribution to ensure smooth flow of materials and products.

Key Responsibilities
• Manage procurement, vendor relationships, and sourcing strategies
• Oversee inventory management and logistics operations
• Optimize supply chain processes and reduce costs
• Ensure timely delivery of materials and products
• Analyze supply chain data and performance metrics
• Manage risks and disruptions in supply chain
• Collaborate with production, planning, and sales teams
• Implement digital supply chain solutions
Required Skills
• Knowledge of supply chain and logistics management
• Vendor management and negotiation skills
• Data analysis and forecasting
• ERP systems (SAP, Oracle)
• Strategic planning and problem-solving
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Industrial Engineering (MBA preferred)
• 8–15 years of experience
