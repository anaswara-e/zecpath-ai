Job Summary
A Manufacturing Consultant helps organizations improve production processes, efficiency,
and cost-effectiveness through process optimization and best practices.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze manufacturing processes and identify inefficiencies
• Recommend process improvements and cost reduction strategies
• Implement Lean Manufacturing and Six Sigma practices
• Optimize production workflows and layouts
• Improve quality and reduce defects
• Train teams on best manufacturing practices
• Monitor performance and provide ongoing support
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of manufacturing processes
• Lean, Six Sigma, and process optimization
• Data analysis and problem-solving
• Communication and consulting skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 8–15+ years of experience
Work Model
• Consulting contracts / Advisory roles
