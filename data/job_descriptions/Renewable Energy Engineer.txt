Job Summary

A Renewable Energy Engineer is responsible for designing, developing, and implementing
systems that generate energy from renewable sources such as solar, wind, hydro, and
biomass. The role focuses on sustainability, efficiency, and reducing environmental impact.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and develop renewable energy systems and projects
• Evaluate feasibility and performance of renewable technologies
• Conduct site assessments and energy yield analysis
• Optimize system efficiency and energy output
• Collaborate with project teams for installation and commissioning
• Ensure compliance with environmental and regulatory standards
• Analyze energy data and prepare technical reports
• Stay updated with emerging renewable technologies
Required Skills
• Knowledge of renewable energy systems (solar, wind, etc.)
• Understanding of energy conversion and power systems
• Data analysis and simulation skills
• Project management and coordination
• Sustainability and environmental awareness
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Energy Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
