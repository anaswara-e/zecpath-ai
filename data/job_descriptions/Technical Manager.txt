Job Summary
A Technical Manager leads engineering teams and oversees technical projects, ensuring that
solutions are technically sound, efficient, and aligned with business goals.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead and manage engineering teams and technical projects
• Review and approve technical designs and solutions
• Ensure projects meet technical specifications and quality standards
• Provide technical guidance and mentorship to team members
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams (production, QA, R&D)

• Drive innovation and continuous improvement initiatives
• Manage technical risks and problem resolution
• Prepare technical reports and presentations for stakeholders
Required Skills
• Strong technical expertise in mechanical engineering
• Leadership and team management
• Problem-solving and decision-making
• Communication and stakeholder management
• Project management skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (MBA preferred)
• 8–12 years of experience
