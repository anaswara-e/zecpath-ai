Job Summary
An ADAS Engineer works on advanced vehicle safety and automation systems such as
autonomous driving, collision avoidance, and driver assistance technologies.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and test ADAS features (lane assist, adaptive cruise control, etc.)
• Work with sensors (camera, radar, LiDAR)
• Integrate software and hardware systems
• Perform system validation and testing
• Analyze real-world driving data
• Ensure compliance with safety standards
Required Skills
• Knowledge of embedded systems and automotive electronics
• Programming (Python, C++, MATLAB)
• Understanding of sensors and control systems
• Data analysis and AI basics (preferred)

Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Electronics / Mechatronics
• 2–6 years of experience
