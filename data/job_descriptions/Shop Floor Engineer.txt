Job Summary
A Shop Floor Engineer directly supervises manufacturing activities on the production floor,
ensuring smooth operations, workforce coordination, and adherence to production targets.
Key Responsibilities
• Supervise daily shop floor operations and workers
• Ensure production targets and timelines are met
• Monitor machine performance and operator efficiency
• Handle real-time troubleshooting of production issues
• Ensure proper use of tools, machines, and safety equipment
• Coordinate with maintenance and quality teams
• Maintain production logs and shift reports
• Implement lean manufacturing practices
Required Skills
• Strong understanding of shop floor operations
• Knowledge of machining, welding, and assembly processes
• Leadership and team management skills
• Quick decision-making ability
Qualifications

• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• 0–3 years experience
