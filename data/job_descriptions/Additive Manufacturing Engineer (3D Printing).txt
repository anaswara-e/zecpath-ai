Job Summary
An Additive Manufacturing Engineer specializes in designing and producing components
using 3D printing technologies, focusing on rapid prototyping, customization, and advanced
manufacturing techniques.
Key Responsibilities
• Design parts for additive manufacturing (DfAM)
• Operate and maintain 3D printing machines
• Select appropriate materials and printing methods
• Optimize designs for strength, weight, and cost
• Conduct post-processing and quality checks
• Collaborate with design and production teams
• Research and implement new AM technologies
Required Skills
• Knowledge of 3D printing technologies (FDM, SLS, SLA)

• CAD tools and design optimization
• Materials science and manufacturing processes
• Problem-solving and innovation
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 2–6 years of experience
