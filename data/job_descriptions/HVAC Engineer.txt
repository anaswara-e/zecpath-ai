Job Summary
An HVAC Engineer designs, installs, and maintains heating, ventilation, and air conditioning
systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications, ensuring thermal comfort
and energy efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Design HVAC systems (heating, cooling, ventilation)
• Perform load calculations and system sizing
• Select equipment such as chillers, compressors, and ducts
• Oversee installation and commissioning of HVAC systems
• Ensure compliance with safety and environmental standards
• Optimize energy efficiency and system performance
• Conduct troubleshooting and maintenance planning
• Prepare technical drawings and documentation
Required Skills

• Knowledge of HVAC design principles and standards
• Experience with load calculation software (HAP, TRACE)
• Understanding of thermodynamics and fluid flow
• Problem-solving and project coordination skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
