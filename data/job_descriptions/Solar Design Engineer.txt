Job Summary
A Solar Design Engineer specializes in designing photovoltaic (PV) systems for residential,
commercial, and industrial applications, ensuring optimal performance and cost efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Design solar PV systems (rooftop and ground-mounted)
• Perform site analysis and solar resource assessment
• Calculate system size, panel layout, and energy output
• Select components (solar panels, inverters, mounting systems)
• Prepare technical drawings and system layouts
• Ensure compliance with electrical and safety standards
• Support installation, testing, and commissioning

• Optimize system performance and cost efficiency
Required Skills
• Knowledge of solar PV systems and design principles
• Experience with design tools (PVsyst, AutoCAD, Helioscope)
• Understanding of electrical integration and grid systems
• Analytical and problem-solving skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Electrical Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
