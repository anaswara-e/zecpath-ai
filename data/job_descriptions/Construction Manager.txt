Job Summary
A Construction Manager oversees on-site construction activities, ensuring projects are
executed efficiently, safely, and according to design specifications and schedules.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage day-to-day construction site operations
• Supervise contractors, engineers, and site teams
• Ensure project execution as per drawings, standards, and timelines
• Monitor site safety and enforce compliance with regulations
• Coordinate with project managers, clients, and stakeholders
• Manage materials, equipment, and workforce
• Track progress and prepare site reports
• Resolve on-site technical and operational issues
• Ensure quality control and inspection processes
• Optimize construction processes for efficiency and cost
Required Skills
• Strong site management and leadership skills
• Knowledge of construction processes and safety standards
• Resource and workforce management
• Problem-solving and decision-making
• Communication and coordination skills

Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• 10–18 years of experience
