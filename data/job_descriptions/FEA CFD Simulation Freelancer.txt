Job Summary
An FEA/CFD Simulation Freelancer provides advanced simulation services to analyze and
optimize product designs using computational tools.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform structural, thermal, and fluid simulations
• Develop simulation models and validate results
• Analyze stress, vibration, heat transfer, and fluid flow
• Provide design optimization recommendations
• Prepare simulation reports and technical documentation
• Collaborate with clients on design improvements
Required Skills
• Expertise in simulation tools (ANSYS, Abaqus, Fluent, OpenFOAM)
• Strong knowledge of mechanics and fluid dynamics
• Data analysis and interpretation
• Attention to detail and accuracy
Qualifications

• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 4–10+ years of experience
Work Model
• Freelance / Remote / High-value projects
