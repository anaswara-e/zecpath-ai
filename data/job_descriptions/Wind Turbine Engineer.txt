Job Summary
A Wind Turbine Engineer is responsible for designing, installing, and maintaining wind
energy systems, ensuring efficient and reliable power generation from wind resources.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and develop wind turbine components and systems
• Conduct wind resource assessment and site analysis
• Monitor turbine performance and optimize energy output
• Support installation and commissioning of wind turbines
• Diagnose and troubleshoot mechanical issues
• Ensure compliance with safety and environmental standards
• Collaborate with maintenance and operations teams
• Analyze data and improve turbine efficiency
Required Skills
• Knowledge of wind energy systems and aerodynamics
• Understanding of mechanical systems and rotating equipment
• Data analysis and performance monitoring
• Problem-solving and troubleshooting skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Renewable Energy Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
