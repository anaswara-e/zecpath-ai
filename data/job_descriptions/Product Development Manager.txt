Job Summary
A Product Development Manager is responsible for overseeing the entire product lifecycle—
from concept to market launch—ensuring alignment with business goals, customer needs,
and engineering feasibility.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage product development lifecycle (concept → design → launch)
• Lead cross-functional teams (engineering, marketing, production)

• Define product requirements and specifications
• Drive innovation and new product development strategies
• Ensure product quality, cost, and timeline targets are met
• Conduct market research and competitor analysis
• Manage budgets and resource allocation
• Oversee prototyping, testing, and validation
Required Skills
• Strong product development and lifecycle management knowledge
• Leadership and team management
• Business and market understanding
• Project management and strategic thinking
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (MBA is a plus)
• 8–15 years of experience
