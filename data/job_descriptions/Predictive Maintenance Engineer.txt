Job Summary
A Predictive Maintenance Engineer uses data, sensors, and advanced analytics to predict
equipment failures before they occur. The role focuses on minimizing downtime, extending
asset life, and optimizing maintenance strategies using condition-based monitoring and
digital tools.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and implement predictive maintenance strategies for critical assets
• Analyze equipment data (vibration, temperature, pressure, oil condition, etc.)
• Use IoT sensors and monitoring systems to track equipment health
• Identify early signs of failure and recommend corrective actions
• Integrate predictive maintenance tools with CMMS/ERP systems
• Collaborate with maintenance and reliability teams
• Build predictive models using data analytics and machine learning (if applicable)
• Generate reports and dashboards for asset performance and failure prediction
• Optimize maintenance schedules based on real-time condition data
Required Skills
• Knowledge of predictive maintenance techniques and tools
• Experience with condition monitoring systems and IoT platforms
• Data analysis and interpretation (Excel, Python – added advantage)
• Understanding of mechanical systems and failure modes
• Familiarity with CMMS/ERP systems
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 4–8 years of experience
