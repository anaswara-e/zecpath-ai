Job Summary
A Marine Engineer is responsible for the operation, maintenance, and repair of mechanical
systems on ships and offshore vessels, ensuring safe and efficient maritime operations.
Key Responsibilities
• Operate and maintain ship machinery (engines, boilers, pumps, compressors)
• Monitor engine performance and fuel efficiency
• Perform routine maintenance and emergency repairs
• Troubleshoot mechanical failures onboard
• Ensure compliance with maritime safety and environmental regulations
• Maintain engine room logs and technical documentation
• Coordinate with onboard crew and technical teams
• Support dry docking and overhaul activities
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of marine engines and ship systems
• Troubleshooting and maintenance skills
• Understanding of maritime safety standards (SOLAS, MARPOL)
• Ability to work in offshore/onboard environments
• Problem-solving and teamwork
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Marine / Mechanical Engineering
• Marine certification (CoC – Certificate of Competency preferred)
• 2–6 years of experience
