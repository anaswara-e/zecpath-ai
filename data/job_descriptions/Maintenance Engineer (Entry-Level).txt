Job Summary
A Maintenance Engineer is responsible for ensuring the smooth operation of machinery and
equipment by performing preventive maintenance, troubleshooting breakdowns, and
supporting reliability improvements within the plant.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform preventive and corrective maintenance on machines and equipment
• Diagnose and troubleshoot mechanical issues and breakdowns
• Assist in installation and commissioning of new machinery
• Monitor equipment performance and identify potential failures
• Maintain maintenance logs, reports, and documentation
• Coordinate with production teams to minimize downtime
• Follow safety standards and maintenance procedures
• Support continuous improvement initiatives for equipment reliability
Required Skills
• Basic knowledge of mechanical systems and industrial equipment
• Understanding of maintenance practices (preventive & corrective)
• Problem-solving and troubleshooting skills
• Familiarity with tools and maintenance equipment
• Basic knowledge of hydraulics and pneumatics
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• 0–2 years of experience
