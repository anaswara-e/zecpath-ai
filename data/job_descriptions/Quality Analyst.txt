Job Summary
A Quality Analyst focuses on analyzing quality data, identifying trends, and driving
improvements using statistical tools and performance metrics.
Key Responsibilities
• Collect and analyze quality data from production processes
• Identify trends, defects, and areas for improvement
• Generate quality reports and dashboards
• Support process improvement initiatives using data insights
• Use statistical tools for quality analysis (SPC, Six Sigma tools)
• Collaborate with QA, QC, and production teams
• Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs)
• Ensure compliance with quality standards

Required Skills
• Strong data analysis and statistical skills
• Knowledge of quality tools (SPC, Six Sigma basics)
• Proficiency in Excel, Minitab, or similar tools
• Attention to detail and analytical thinking
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Industrial Engineering
• 3–6 years of experience
