Job Summary
A Production Manager is responsible for overseeing daily manufacturing operations, ensuring
production targets are met while maintaining quality, safety, and cost efficiency. This role
manages teams, resources, and production planning.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan, organize, and control daily production activities
• Ensure production targets, quality standards, and deadlines are achieved
• Manage workforce, shifts, and shop floor operations
• Monitor machine utilization and production efficiency (OEE)
• Identify bottlenecks and implement process improvements
• Coordinate with quality, maintenance, and supply chain teams
• Ensure compliance with safety, health, and environmental regulations
• Prepare production reports and performance metrics
• Drive cost reduction and waste minimization initiatives
Required Skills
• Strong production planning and control knowledge
• Leadership and team management
• Knowledge of Lean Manufacturing & Six Sigma
• Problem-solving and decision-making skills
• Familiarity with ERP systems (SAP/Oracle)
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 8–12 years of experience
