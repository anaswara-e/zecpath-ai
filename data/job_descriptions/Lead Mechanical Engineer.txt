Job Summary
A Lead Mechanical Engineer oversees multiple engineering projects, providing technical
direction, ensuring quality, and managing engineering teams to deliver successful outcomes.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead and manage mechanical engineering teams
• Provide technical guidance and decision-making support
• Oversee design, analysis, and development activities
• Ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget
• Coordinate with stakeholders and cross-functional teams
• Drive innovation and process improvements
• Ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations
• Review and approve engineering deliverables
Required Skills
• Strong leadership and project management skills
• Expertise in mechanical design and engineering principles
• Knowledge of project management tools and methodologies
• Excellent communication and stakeholder management
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 8–12 years of experience
