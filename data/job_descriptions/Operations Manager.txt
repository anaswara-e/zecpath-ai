Job Summary
An Operations Manager is responsible for overseeing end-to-end manufacturing operations,
ensuring efficiency, productivity, and alignment with business goals across multiple
departments or units.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage overall operations including production, supply chain, and logistics
• Develop operational strategies to improve efficiency and reduce costs
• Monitor performance metrics and drive continuous improvement
• Ensure optimal utilization of resources (manpower, machines, materials)
• Lead cross-functional teams and coordinate between departments
• Implement Lean, Six Sigma, and operational excellence initiatives
• Manage budgets and operational expenditures
• Ensure compliance with quality and safety standards
• Drive digital transformation and automation initiatives
Required Skills

• Strong operations and business management skills
• Leadership and stakeholder management
• Data-driven decision-making
• Knowledge of supply chain and logistics
• Expertise in ERP and analytics tools
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (MBA preferred)
• 10–15+ years of experience
