Job Summary
An Engineering Manager is responsible for managing engineering operations, teams, and
project execution while aligning technical activities with organizational goals.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage engineering teams and ensure project delivery
• Plan and allocate resources effectively
• Oversee design, development, and testing processes
• Monitor performance metrics and improve efficiency
• Collaborate with business and operations teams
• Ensure compliance with quality and safety standards
• Drive innovation and product development
• Manage budgets and timelines
Required Skills
• Leadership and organizational skills
• Strong engineering and technical knowledge
• Project and resource management
• Strategic thinking and decision-making
• Communication and collaboration
Qualifications

• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (MBA preferred)
• 8–15 years of experience
