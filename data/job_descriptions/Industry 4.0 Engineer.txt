Job Summary
An Industry 4.0 Engineer drives digital transformation in manufacturing by implementing
smart technologies such as IoT, automation, data analytics, and cyber-physical systems.
Key Responsibilities

• Implement smart manufacturing solutions and IoT systems
• Integrate machines with digital platforms (IIoT)
• Analyze production data for optimization
• Develop connected factory systems
• Collaborate with IT, automation, and production teams
• Improve efficiency using real-time monitoring
• Support digital transformation initiatives
Required Skills
• Knowledge of Industry 4.0 technologies (IoT, AI, automation)
• Data analytics and system integration
• Programming and networking basics
• Understanding of manufacturing systems
• Problem-solving and innovation mindset
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Mechatronics Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
