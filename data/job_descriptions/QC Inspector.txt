Job Summary
A QC Inspector is responsible for inspecting materials and products to ensure they meet
required quality standards. This is a hands-on role focused on measurement, testing, and
defect identification.
Key Responsibilities
• Inspect incoming materials, in-process parts, and finished products
• Perform dimensional and visual inspections using measuring tools
• Verify compliance with drawings, tolerances, and specifications
• Identify defects and non-conformities in products
• Record inspection results and prepare reports
• Work closely with production teams to resolve quality issues
• Ensure proper calibration and use of inspection equipment
• Follow safety and quality procedures
Required Skills
• Knowledge of inspection tools (vernier caliper, micrometer, height gauge)
• Understanding of engineering drawings and tolerances
• Strong attention to detail and accuracy
• Basic documentation and reporting skills
Qualifications
• Diploma / ITI / B.E. in Mechanical Engineering
• 0–2 years of experience
