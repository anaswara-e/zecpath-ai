Job Summary
A Maintenance Manager is responsible for overseeing all maintenance activities within a
plant or facility, ensuring maximum equipment uptime, efficient resource utilization, and
compliance with safety and regulatory standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan, organize, and manage preventive, predictive, and corrective maintenance
activities
• Ensure high equipment availability and minimal downtime
• Lead and supervise maintenance teams (engineers, technicians, planners)
• Develop and implement maintenance strategies and schedules
• Monitor maintenance KPIs such as MTBF, MTTR, and OEE
• Coordinate with production, quality, and engineering teams
• Manage maintenance budgets, costs, and spare parts inventory
• Ensure compliance with safety, health, and environmental standards
• Drive continuous improvement and reliability initiatives
• Oversee installation, commissioning, and upgrades of equipment
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of maintenance and reliability practices
• Leadership and team management
• Budgeting and cost control
• Problem-solving and decision-making
• Familiarity with CMMS/ERP systems (SAP PM)
• Knowledge of predictive maintenance technologies

Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 8–12 years of experience
