Job Summary
A Six Sigma Engineer is responsible for improving process quality by reducing defects and
variability using Six Sigma methodologies and statistical analysis tools.
Key Responsibilities

• Lead Six Sigma projects using DMAIC methodology
• Analyze processes to identify defects and root causes
• Use statistical tools for data-driven decision-making
• Implement process improvements to reduce variation
• Monitor and control process performance
• Train teams on Six Sigma tools and methodologies
• Collaborate with quality and production teams
• Ensure continuous improvement and compliance with standards
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of Six Sigma (DMAIC, DMADV)
• Statistical analysis tools (Minitab, Excel)
• Root cause analysis techniques
• Data interpretation and reporting skills
• Problem-solving mindset
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Industrial Engineering
• 3–8 years of experience
• Six Sigma Green Belt / Black Belt certification (preferred)
