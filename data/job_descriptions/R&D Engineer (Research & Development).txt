Job Summary

An R&D Engineer focuses on innovation, developing new technologies, products, and
processes through research, experimentation, and testing.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct research and feasibility studies for new concepts
• Develop prototypes and test new designs
• Analyze data and improve product performance
• Work on innovation and patentable ideas
• Collaborate with design and production teams
• Stay updated with latest industry trends and technologies
• Prepare technical reports and documentation
Required Skills
• Strong analytical and research skills
• Knowledge of simulation and testing methods
• Creativity and innovation mindset
• Programming knowledge (Python/MATLAB – optional)
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–6 years experience
