Job Summary
An Engine Development Engineer focuses on designing, testing, and optimizing internal
combustion engines or hybrid powertrains for performance, efficiency, and emissions.
Key Responsibilities

• Develop and test engine components and systems
• Optimize engine performance and fuel efficiency
• Conduct engine testing and validation
• Analyze emissions and ensure regulatory compliance
• Collaborate with design and testing teams
• Troubleshoot engine issues and implement improvements
Required Skills
• Knowledge of IC engines and thermodynamics
• Experience with engine testing tools
• Data analysis and performance optimization
• Understanding of emission standards
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Automotive Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
