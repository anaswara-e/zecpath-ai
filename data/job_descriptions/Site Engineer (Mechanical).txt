Job Summary
A Site Engineer (Mechanical) is responsible for supervising and executing mechanical works
at construction sites, ensuring projects are completed as per design, schedule, and quality
standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Supervise installation of mechanical systems (HVAC, piping, machinery)
• Coordinate with contractors, vendors, and project teams
• Ensure work is executed as per drawings and specifications
• Monitor project progress and report status
• Conduct site inspections and quality checks
• Ensure compliance with safety regulations and standards
• Manage materials, manpower, and site resources

• Resolve on-site technical issues
Required Skills
• Knowledge of construction and mechanical systems
• Understanding of drawings and project specifications
• Site management and coordination skills
• Problem-solving and decision-making
• Basic project management skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• 2–6 years of experience
