Job Summary
A CAD Freelancer specializes in creating 2D drawings and 3D models for clients across
industries, supporting design, drafting, and documentation needs.
Key Responsibilities
• Create detailed 2D drawings and 3D CAD models
• Convert sketches or concepts into technical drawings
• Modify and update existing designs
• Ensure accuracy and adherence to design standards
• Deliver drawings within deadlines
• Collaborate with designers and engineers remotely
Required Skills
• Proficiency in CAD tools (AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Fusion 360)
• Knowledge of drafting standards and GD&T basics
• Attention to detail and precision
• Time management and client handling
Qualifications
• Diploma / B.E. in Mechanical Engineering
• 2–6 years of experience
Work Model
• Freelance / Remote / Project-based
