Job Summary
A Vehicle Design Engineer focuses on designing vehicle components and systems, ensuring
functionality, aesthetics, manufacturability, and cost efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Create 3D models and detailed drawings of vehicle components
• Design body structures, chassis, and interior systems
• Ensure design meets performance, safety, and cost requirements
• Collaborate with CAE and testing teams for validation

• Optimize designs for manufacturability and assembly
• Manage design changes and documentation
Required Skills
• Advanced CAD tools (CATIA, NX, Creo)
• Knowledge of materials and manufacturing processes
• Understanding of GD&T
• Creativity and design thinking
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Automotive Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
