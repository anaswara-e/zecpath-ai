Job Summary
A Manufacturing Engineer is responsible for designing, improving, and optimizing
manufacturing processes to enhance productivity, reduce costs, and maintain product quality.
This role bridges design and production.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and optimize manufacturing processes and workflows
• Evaluate production methods and recommend improvements
• Implement new technologies, tools, and automation systems
• Collaborate with design teams for manufacturability (DFM/DFA)
• Analyze production data to improve efficiency and reduce waste
• Support new product introduction (NPI) into manufacturing
• Ensure compliance with quality and safety standards
• Train production staff on new processes and equipment
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of machining, fabrication, and assembly processes
• Understanding of Lean Manufacturing and Six Sigma principles
• Familiarity with CAD tools and process simulation
• Data analysis and problem-solving skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
