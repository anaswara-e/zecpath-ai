Job Summary
An Operations Manager oversees day-to-day operations in manufacturing or industrial
facilities, ensuring efficiency, productivity, and cost-effectiveness.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage production, maintenance, and operational processes
• Optimize workflows and improve productivity
• Monitor KPIs such as output, efficiency, and downtime
• Ensure quality, safety, and compliance standards
• Manage workforce, resources, and budgets
• Implement continuous improvement initiatives
• Coordinate with supply chain, quality, and engineering teams
• Resolve operational issues and bottlenecks
Required Skills
• Strong operational and process management skills
• Leadership and team management
• Data-driven decision-making
• Problem-solving and efficiency improvement
• Knowledge of manufacturing systems
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (MBA preferred)
• 8–15 years of experience
