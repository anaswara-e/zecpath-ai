Job Summary
A CAD Engineer specializes in creating precise technical drawings and 3D models for
mechanical components. The role bridges design concepts with manufacturing requirements.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop 2D drawings and 3D models using CAD software
• Convert conceptual designs into detailed technical drawings
• Ensure drawings meet industry standards and tolerances
• Revise drawings based on engineering changes
• Collaborate with design and production teams
• Maintain CAD libraries and documentation
Required Skills
• Strong proficiency in AutoCAD, SolidWorks, CATIA, or Creo
• Knowledge of drafting standards and GD&T
• Good visualization and technical drawing skills
• Understanding of manufacturing processes
Qualifications

• Diploma / B.E. in Mechanical Engineering
• 0–2 years experience
