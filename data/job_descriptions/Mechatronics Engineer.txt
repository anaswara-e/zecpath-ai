Job Summary
A Mechatronics Engineer designs and develops integrated systems that combine mechanical,
electrical, and software components. The role focuses on building smart machines, automated
systems, and robotics solutions for industrial and commercial applications.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and develop mechatronic systems combining mechanical, electrical, and
control elements
• Integrate sensors, actuators, PLCs, and embedded systems
• Develop and test automated machinery and robotic systems
• Collaborate with mechanical, electrical, and software teams
• Troubleshoot and optimize system performance
• Support system installation, testing, and commissioning
• Develop technical documentation and system specifications
• Improve system efficiency, reliability, and performance
Required Skills

• Strong knowledge of mechanical, electrical, and control systems
• Experience with PLC programming and embedded systems
• Understanding of robotics and automation systems
• Programming skills (Python, C/C++, MATLAB)
• Problem-solving and system integration skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Mechatronics / Electronics Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
