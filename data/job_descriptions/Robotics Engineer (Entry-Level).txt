Job Summary
A Robotics Engineer is responsible for developing, programming, and maintaining robotic
systems used in manufacturing, assembly, and automation applications.
Key Responsibilities
• Program and operate industrial robots (welding, assembly, pick-and-place)

• Assist in robot integration with production lines
• Work with robotic arms, sensors, and vision systems
• Troubleshoot robotic system issues
• Support testing and validation of robotic applications
• Collaborate with automation and production teams
• Maintain robot programs and documentation
• Optimize robotic processes for efficiency and accuracy
Required Skills
• Basic knowledge of robotics and automation systems
• Programming skills (Python, C++, or robot-specific languages)
• Understanding of kinematics and control systems
• Familiarity with robotic platforms (ABB, FANUC, KUKA)
• Problem-solving and analytical thinking
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Mechatronics / Robotics Engineering
• 0–2 years of experience
