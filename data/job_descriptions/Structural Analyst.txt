Job Summary

A Structural Analyst evaluates the strength, durability, and safety of aircraft structures using
advanced simulation and analysis techniques.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform structural analysis using FEA tools
• Analyze stress, fatigue, and failure modes of components
• Validate designs against safety and performance requirements
• Conduct load and strength calculations
• Collaborate with design engineers for structural optimization
• Prepare analysis reports and documentation
• Support certification and testing activities
Required Skills
• Expertise in FEA tools (ANSYS, Abaqus, HyperMesh)
• Strong knowledge of mechanics of materials
• Analytical and data interpretation skills
• Attention to detail
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical/Aerospace Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
