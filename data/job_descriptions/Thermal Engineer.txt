Job Summary
A Thermal Engineer is responsible for designing, analyzing, and optimizing thermal systems
involving heat transfer, energy conversion, and temperature control in applications such as
power plants, engines, and industrial processes.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and analyze thermal systems (boilers, heat exchangers, cooling systems)
• Perform heat transfer and thermodynamic calculations
• Conduct thermal simulations and performance analysis
• Optimize system efficiency and reduce energy losses
• Support design and operation of power generation systems
• Collaborate with design, production, and maintenance teams
• Identify and resolve thermal performance issues
• Prepare technical reports and documentation
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of thermodynamics and heat transfer
• Experience with simulation tools (ANSYS, MATLAB, CFD tools)
• Analytical and problem-solving skills
• Understanding of energy systems and power plants
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
