Job Summary
A Pipeline Engineer is responsible for designing, analyzing, and maintaining pipelines used
for transporting oil, gas, water, or chemicals. The role ensures safe, efficient, and reliable
fluid transport systems.

Key Responsibilities
• Design pipeline systems and layouts
• Perform stress analysis and fluid flow calculations
• Ensure compliance with industry standards and safety regulations
• Monitor pipeline performance and integrity
• Conduct risk assessments and failure analysis
• Support installation, testing, and commissioning of pipelines
• Collaborate with civil, mechanical, and safety teams
• Prepare technical documentation and reports
Required Skills
• Knowledge of fluid mechanics and pipeline design
• Familiarity with pipeline standards (ASME, API)
• Experience with simulation and design tools
• Risk assessment and problem-solving skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
