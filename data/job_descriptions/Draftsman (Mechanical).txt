Job Summary
A Mechanical Draftsman prepares detailed technical drawings and layouts based on engineer
specifications. This role focuses heavily on drafting accuracy and documentation.
Key Responsibilities
• Create detailed mechanical drawings and layouts
• Interpret rough sketches and convert them into technical drawings
• Ensure compliance with drafting standards and codes
• Update drawings as per design changes
• Maintain drawing records and documentation
• Coordinate with engineers for clarifications
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of AutoCAD or similar drafting tools
• Understanding of engineering drawing standards
• Attention to detail and precision
• Basic mechanical knowledge
Qualifications
• ITI / Diploma in Mechanical Engineering or Drafting
• 0–2 years experience
