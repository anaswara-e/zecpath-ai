Job Summary
An Automotive Design Engineer develops and designs vehicle components and systems,
ensuring performance, safety, and compliance with automotive standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Design automotive components (chassis, engine parts, body systems)
• Develop CAD models and perform simulations
• Conduct testing and validation of vehicle systems
• Collaborate with manufacturing and testing teams
• Ensure compliance with automotive regulations and safety standards
• Optimize designs for performance, cost, and efficiency
• Support prototype development and testing

Required Skills
• CAD tools (CATIA, Creo, NX)
• Knowledge of automotive systems and standards
• Basics of CAE (FEA/CFD)
• Understanding of materials and manufacturing
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical/Automotive Engineering
• 3–8 years experience
