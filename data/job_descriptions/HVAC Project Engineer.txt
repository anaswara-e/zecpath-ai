Job Summary
An HVAC Project Engineer specializes in managing HVAC system projects, from design
coordination to installation and commissioning, ensuring efficient climate control systems.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan and execute HVAC projects (commercial/industrial)
• Coordinate with design teams for system layouts
• Oversee installation, testing, and commissioning
• Ensure compliance with HVAC standards and codes
• Manage project schedules, costs, and resources
• Coordinate with contractors and vendors
• Conduct performance testing and optimization
• Prepare project documentation and reports
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of HVAC systems and design
• Project management and coordination
• Familiarity with HVAC software/tools
• Problem-solving and troubleshooting
• Communication and leadership
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–8 years of experience
