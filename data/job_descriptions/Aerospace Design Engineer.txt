Job Summary
An Aerospace Design Engineer is responsible for designing aircraft structures, components,
and systems with a focus on performance, safety, and regulatory compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Design aircraft structures and mechanical systems
• Perform structural and aerodynamic analysis
• Develop CAD models and detailed engineering drawings
• Conduct simulations and validation tests
• Ensure compliance with aerospace standards (FAA, EASA, etc.)
• Collaborate with multidisciplinary engineering teams
• Support prototype development and flight testing
Required Skills
• CAD tools (CATIA, NX)
• Strong knowledge of aerodynamics and structural mechanics
• Experience with FEA/CFD tools
• High attention to detail and precision
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical/Aerospace Engineering
• 3–8 years experience
