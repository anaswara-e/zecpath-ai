Job Summary
An NVH Engineer specializes in analyzing and reducing noise, vibration, and harshness in
vehicles to improve comfort, durability, and performance.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct NVH testing and analysis on vehicle components
• Identify sources of noise and vibration
• Perform modal and frequency analysis
• Recommend design changes to reduce NVH issues
• Use simulation tools for NVH prediction
• Collaborate with design and testing teams
• Validate improvements through testing
Required Skills
• Knowledge of vibration and acoustics
• Experience with NVH tools (LMS, HyperWorks, ANSYS)
• Strong analytical skills
• Understanding of vehicle systems
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering

• 3–7 years of experience
