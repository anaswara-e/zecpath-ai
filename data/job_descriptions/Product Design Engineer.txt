Job Summary
A Product Design Engineer focuses on designing user-centric products by combining
engineering principles with innovation, usability, and cost optimization.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and develop new products from concept to launch
• Conduct feasibility studies and design validation
• Work on prototyping and product testing
• Optimize product performance, cost, and manufacturability
• Collaborate with R&D, marketing, and production teams
• Prepare BOM, technical specifications, and documentation
• Ensure compliance with regulatory and safety standards
Required Skills
• Strong CAD and product design skills
• Knowledge of materials and manufacturing techniques
• Creativity and innovation mindset

• Understanding of product lifecycle management (PLM)
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering or Product Design
• 3–6 years of experience
