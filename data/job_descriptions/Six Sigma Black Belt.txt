Job Summary
A Six Sigma Black Belt is responsible for leading high-impact process improvement projects
using Six Sigma methodologies. This role focuses on reducing defects, improving quality,
and driving operational excellence across the organization.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead cross-functional Six Sigma projects using DMAIC/DMADV
• Identify process inefficiencies and improvement opportunities
• Perform advanced statistical analysis and data modeling
• Drive defect reduction and process optimization initiatives
• Mentor and train Green Belts and project teams
• Monitor and report project performance and savings
• Collaborate with leadership to align improvement projects with business goals
• Implement continuous improvement culture across the organization
Required Skills
• Strong expertise in Six Sigma methodologies and tools
• Advanced statistical analysis (Minitab, JMP, Excel)
• Leadership and project management skills
• Root cause analysis and problem-solving
• Data-driven decision-making
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Industrial Engineering
• 6–10+ years of experience
• Six Sigma Black Belt Certification (mandatory)
