Job Summary
An Aerospace Engineer designs, develops, and tests aircraft, spacecraft, and related systems.
The role ensures performance, safety, and compliance with aviation and defense standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and develop aircraft or spacecraft systems and components
• Perform aerodynamic and performance analysis
• Collaborate with multidisciplinary engineering teams
• Ensure compliance with aviation standards and regulations
• Support prototype development and testing
• Analyze system performance and implement improvements
• Prepare technical documentation and reports
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of aerodynamics, propulsion, and structures
• CAD/CAE tools (CATIA, ANSYS, NX)
• Analytical and problem-solving skills
• Understanding of aerospace standards and safety
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Aerospace / Mechanical Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
