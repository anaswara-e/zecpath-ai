Job Summary
A Quality Engineer ensures that manufactured products meet defined quality standards and
specifications. This role involves supporting quality systems, conducting inspections, and
driving basic quality improvements across production processes.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform quality inspections on raw materials, in-process components, and finished
products
• Ensure compliance with engineering drawings, specifications, and standards
• Assist in developing and maintaining quality control procedures and documentation
• Support root cause analysis for defects and implement corrective actions
• Collaborate with production and design teams to resolve quality issues
• Maintain inspection records, reports, and quality metrics
• Participate in internal audits and quality improvement initiatives
• Ensure adherence to ISO and company quality standards

Required Skills
• Basic understanding of quality control tools and techniques
• Knowledge of engineering drawings and GD&T
• Familiarity with inspection instruments (vernier caliper, micrometer, gauges)
• Problem-solving and analytical skills
• Attention to detail
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 0–2 years of experience
