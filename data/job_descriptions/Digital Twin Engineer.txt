Job Summary
A Digital Twin Engineer creates virtual replicas of physical systems (machines, plants, or
products) to simulate, monitor, and optimize real-world performance in real-time.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop digital twin models of machines and systems
• Integrate IoT data for real-time monitoring
• Simulate system behavior and predict failures
• Optimize performance using virtual testing
• Collaborate with design, operations, and IT teams
• Use simulation tools and data analytics platforms
• Support decision-making using digital insights
Required Skills
• Simulation tools (ANSYS, Siemens NX, MATLAB)
• IoT integration and data analytics
• Knowledge of mechanical systems and modeling
• Programming (Python, C++)
• Systems thinking
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Mechatronics Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
