Job Summary
A Service Engineer is responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing equipment at
customer or client locations. This role requires strong technical skills along with customer
interaction and field support.
Key Responsibilities
• Install, test, and commission machinery at client sites
• Diagnose and repair equipment faults and breakdowns
• Provide on-site and remote technical support to customers
• Conduct routine maintenance and service inspections
• Train customers on equipment usage and maintenance
• Maintain service reports and documentation
• Ensure timely response to service requests and issues
• Coordinate with internal teams for spare parts and technical support
Required Skills
• Strong troubleshooting and diagnostic skills
• Knowledge of mechanical systems and equipment
• Customer handling and communication skills
• Willingness to travel for field service
• Basic knowledge of electrical systems (added advantage)
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• 0–2 years of experience
