Job Summary

A Supplier Quality Engineer ensures that materials and components supplied by vendors
meet quality standards. This role involves supplier audits, performance monitoring, and
quality improvement initiatives.
Key Responsibilities
• Evaluate and qualify new suppliers based on quality standards
• Conduct supplier audits and assessments
• Monitor supplier performance (quality, delivery, compliance)
• Handle supplier-related quality issues and corrective actions
• Ensure incoming materials meet specifications
• Collaborate with procurement and production teams
• Drive supplier quality improvement programs
• Maintain supplier quality documentation and reports
Required Skills
• Knowledge of supplier quality management
• Strong auditing and communication skills
• Understanding of manufacturing and quality processes
• Problem-solving and negotiation skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
