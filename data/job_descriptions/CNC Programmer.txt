Job Summary
A CNC Programmer is responsible for creating, optimizing, and maintaining CNC programs
used in machining operations such as milling, turning, and drilling. The role ensures
precision, efficiency, and quality in manufactured components.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop CNC programs using G-code/M-code for machining operations
• Interpret engineering drawings, CAD models, and technical specifications
• Select appropriate cutting tools, speeds, and feeds
• Set up CNC machines and support operators during production
• Optimize machining processes to reduce cycle time and improve quality
• Troubleshoot machining issues and modify programs as needed
• Ensure compliance with quality standards and tolerances
• Maintain program documentation and revision control
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of CNC machining (milling, turning)
• Proficiency in CAM software (Mastercam, Fusion 360, NX CAM)
• Understanding of GD&T and engineering drawings
• Knowledge of cutting tools and materials
• Problem-solving and attention to detail
Qualifications
• Diploma / B.E. in Mechanical Engineering or Tool Engineering
• 2–6 years of experience
