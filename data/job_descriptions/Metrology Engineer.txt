Job Summary
A Metrology Engineer is responsible for ensuring accuracy and precision in measurement
systems used in manufacturing. This role involves calibration, measurement analysis, and
maintaining measurement standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and maintain measurement and calibration systems
• Ensure accuracy of inspection instruments and equipment
• Perform gauge calibration and measurement system analysis (MSA)
• Work with Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) and advanced tools
• Establish measurement standards and procedures
• Analyze measurement errors and improve accuracy
• Support quality inspections and validation processes
• Maintain calibration records and documentation
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of metrology and measurement techniques
• Experience with CMM, laser measurement, and gauges
• Understanding of GD&T and tolerance analysis

• Data analysis and precision engineering skills
• Attention to detail and accuracy
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 4–8 years of experience
