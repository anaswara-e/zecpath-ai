Job Summary
An Industrial Engineer focuses on improving overall system efficiency by optimizing
workflows, reducing waste, and enhancing productivity using engineering and management
principles.
Key Responsibilities
• Analyze production processes and workflow efficiency
• Perform time and motion studies
• Optimize resource utilization (manpower, machines, materials)
• Implement Lean Manufacturing and continuous improvement initiatives
• Design plant layouts and workflow systems
• Improve productivity and reduce operational costs
• Use data analytics for decision-making
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams
Required Skills

• Strong knowledge of Lean, Six Sigma, and industrial engineering tools
• Data analysis and statistical skills
• Process mapping and workflow optimization
• Problem-solving and critical thinking
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Industrial Engineering
• 3–7 years experience
