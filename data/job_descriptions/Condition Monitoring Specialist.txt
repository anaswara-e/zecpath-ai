Job Summary
A Condition Monitoring Specialist focuses on assessing equipment health using diagnostic
techniques such as vibration analysis, thermography, and oil analysis to detect faults and
prevent failures.
Key Responsibilities

• Perform condition monitoring using vibration analysis, thermography, ultrasound, and
oil analysis
• Collect and interpret data from monitoring equipment
• Diagnose faults such as misalignment, imbalance, wear, and lubrication issues
• Recommend corrective maintenance actions
• Maintain monitoring schedules and inspection routines
• Prepare detailed diagnostic reports and recommendations
• Work closely with maintenance and reliability teams
• Ensure calibration and proper functioning of monitoring instruments
Required Skills
• Expertise in vibration analysis and diagnostic tools
• Knowledge of thermography and lubrication analysis
• Strong analytical and troubleshooting skills
• Understanding of rotating equipment (motors, pumps, turbines)
• Attention to detail and reporting skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
• Certification in vibration analysis (ISO Category I/II – preferred)
