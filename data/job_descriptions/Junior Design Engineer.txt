Job Summary
A Junior Design Engineer supports the design and development of mechanical components
and systems. This role involves creating CAD models, preparing technical drawings, and
assisting in product development.
Key Responsibilities

• Create 2D/3D CAD models using design software
• Prepare detailed engineering drawings and BOM (Bill of Materials)
• Assist in product design and development activities
• Modify designs based on feedback and testing results
• Work with manufacturing teams to ensure design feasibility
• Maintain design documentation and version control
Required Skills
• Proficiency in CAD tools (SolidWorks, CATIA, AutoCAD)
• Understanding of GD&T (Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerancing)
• Basic knowledge of materials and manufacturing processes
• Attention to detail
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• 0–2 years of experience
