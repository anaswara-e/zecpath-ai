Job Summary
A Plant Head is responsible for overall management of a power plant or industrial facility,
ensuring efficient operations, profitability, safety, and compliance with regulatory standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Oversee complete plant operations including production, maintenance, and quality
• Develop and execute operational strategies to achieve business goals
• Manage plant budgets, costs, and financial performance
• Ensure compliance with safety, environmental, and regulatory standards
• Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) such as efficiency, output, and downtime
• Lead and manage cross-functional teams
• Drive continuous improvement and operational excellence initiatives
• Coordinate with stakeholders, vendors, and government authorities
• Ensure optimal utilization of resources (manpower, machinery, materials)
• Lead plant expansion, modernization, and digital transformation projects
Required Skills
• Strong leadership and strategic management
• Deep knowledge of plant operations and energy systems
• Financial and budget management
• Problem-solving and decision-making skills
• Knowledge of regulatory and compliance standards
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (MBA preferred)
• 10–15+ years of experience
