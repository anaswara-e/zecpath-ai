Job Summary
A Design Engineer is responsible for developing mechanical components and systems from
concept to production. The role involves design calculations, CAD modeling, and
collaboration with cross-functional teams to ensure functional and manufacturable designs.

Key Responsibilities
• Develop mechanical designs based on project requirements
• Perform engineering calculations and material selection
• Create 2D/3D CAD models and detailed drawings
• Ensure compliance with industry standards and safety regulations
• Coordinate with manufacturing and quality teams
• Review and validate designs through testing and analysis
• Manage design revisions and documentation
Required Skills
• Strong CAD skills (SolidWorks, CATIA, Creo)
• Knowledge of GD&T and engineering standards
• Understanding of manufacturing processes
• Analytical and problem-solving skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–6 years of experience
