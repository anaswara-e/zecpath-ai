Job Summary
A Project Engineer manages engineering projects from planning to execution, ensuring
timely delivery, cost control, and quality compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan, schedule, and execute engineering projects
• Coordinate with design, procurement, and site teams
• Monitor project timelines, budgets, and resources
• Ensure compliance with technical specifications and standards
• Manage risks and resolve project issues
• Prepare project reports and documentation
• Communicate with clients and stakeholders
• Ensure project quality and safety standards
Required Skills
• Strong project management and coordination skills
• Knowledge of engineering design and execution
• Budgeting and cost control
• Communication and leadership skills
• Problem-solving abilities
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–8 years of experience
