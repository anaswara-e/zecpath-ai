Job Summary
A Maintenance Planner is responsible for planning, scheduling, and coordinating
maintenance activities to ensure efficient resource utilization and minimal equipment
downtime.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan and schedule preventive and corrective maintenance activities
• Develop maintenance schedules and work orders
• Coordinate with maintenance teams, production, and stores
• Ensure availability of spare parts, tools, and manpower
• Maintain maintenance records, schedules, and documentation
• Optimize maintenance workflows and reduce downtime
• Track maintenance KPIs and performance metrics
• Use CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management Systems) for planning and
tracking

Required Skills
• Strong planning and scheduling skills
• Knowledge of maintenance processes and workflows
• Familiarity with CMMS/ERP systems (SAP PM module preferred)
• Coordination and communication skills
• Analytical and organizational abilities
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
