Job Summary
An Automation Lead is responsible for leading automation projects, managing teams, and
ensuring the successful design, implementation, and optimization of automated systems
across manufacturing or industrial environments.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead end-to-end automation projects (design → implementation → commissioning)
• Manage a team of automation engineers and technicians
• Design and review automation system architecture (PLC, SCADA, DCS)
• Ensure integration of automation systems with production processes
• Oversee installation, testing, and commissioning activities
• Identify opportunities for automation and process optimization
• Ensure compliance with safety, quality, and industry standards
• Coordinate with cross-functional teams (production, maintenance, IT)
• Manage project timelines, budgets, and resources
• Drive continuous improvement and digital transformation initiatives
Required Skills
• Strong expertise in industrial automation systems (PLC, SCADA, DCS)
• Leadership and project management skills
• Knowledge of robotics and control systems
• Problem-solving and decision-making abilities
• Familiarity with Industry 4.0 technologies (IoT, smart factories)
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Mechatronics / Electrical Engineering
• 8–12 years of experience
