Job Summary
A Mechanical Design Consultant provides expert design solutions for products, machines,
and systems, working with clients across industries to develop efficient, cost-effective, and
manufacturable designs.
Key Responsibilities
• Design mechanical components and systems based on client requirements
• Create 3D models, assemblies, and detailed engineering drawings
• Conduct design validation and feasibility analysis
• Optimize designs for cost, performance, and manufacturability
• Collaborate with clients and cross-functional teams
• Provide technical advice and design improvements
• Ensure compliance with industry standards and specifications
• Deliver project documentation and reports

Required Skills
• Advanced CAD tools (SolidWorks, CATIA, Creo, NX)
• Strong design fundamentals and GD&T
• Knowledge of materials and manufacturing processes
• Client communication and project management
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 5–10+ years of experience
Work Model
• Freelance / Contract / Remote consulting
