Job Summary
A Simulation Engineer performs advanced engineering analysis using tools like Finite
Element Analysis (FEA) and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) to validate and optimize
designs.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform structural, thermal, and fluid simulations
• Develop and validate simulation models
• Analyze stress, vibration, heat transfer, and fluid flow
• Optimize designs based on simulation results
• Collaborate with design teams to improve product performance
• Prepare simulation reports and documentation
• Ensure accuracy and validation of simulation results
Required Skills

• Expertise in FEA/CFD tools (ANSYS, Abaqus, Fluent, HyperMesh)
• Strong understanding of mechanics, thermodynamics, and fluid mechanics
• Data analysis and interpretation skills
• Programming (Python, MATLAB) – added advantage
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–6 years experience
