Job Summary
A Product Manager is responsible for managing the lifecycle of mechanical products, from
concept to launch, ensuring they meet customer needs and business objectives.
Key Responsibilities
• Define product strategy, roadmap, and vision
• Gather customer requirements and market insights
• Work with engineering teams for product development
• Coordinate product launches and marketing strategies
• Monitor product performance and customer feedback
• Manage product lifecycle (design → launch → improvement)
• Collaborate with sales, marketing, and operations teams
• Ensure product profitability and success

Required Skills
• Product management and market analysis
• Strong communication and leadership skills
• Technical understanding of mechanical products
• Business and strategic thinking
• Project management
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering + MBA (preferred)
• 5–12 years of experience
