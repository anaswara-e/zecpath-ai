Job Summary
A Production Engineer is responsible for managing day-to-day manufacturing operations,
ensuring efficient production processes, optimal resource utilization, and adherence to quality
and safety standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan and execute daily production schedules
• Monitor manufacturing processes and improve efficiency
• Coordinate with design, quality, and maintenance teams
• Ensure machines and equipment are functioning properly
• Identify and resolve production issues and bottlenecks
• Implement process improvements and cost-reduction initiatives
• Maintain production reports and documentation
• Ensure compliance with safety and quality standards
Required Skills
• Knowledge of manufacturing processes (machining, fabrication, assembly)
• Basic understanding of CNC machines and production equipment
• Problem-solving and analytical skills
• Strong coordination and communication skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 0–2 years of experience
