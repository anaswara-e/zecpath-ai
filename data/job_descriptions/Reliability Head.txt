Job Summary
A Reliability Head is a senior leadership role responsible for developing and driving the
organization’s reliability strategy to maximize asset performance, reduce failures, and ensure
long-term operational efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Define and implement enterprise-wide reliability strategy
• Lead reliability engineering and predictive maintenance programs
• Analyze equipment performance and drive failure reduction initiatives
• Oversee condition monitoring and advanced diagnostics
• Establish reliability KPIs and performance benchmarks
• Drive continuous improvement and asset optimization programs
• Collaborate with maintenance, production, and engineering teams
• Lead digital transformation initiatives (predictive analytics, IoT-based monitoring)
• Ensure compliance with industry standards and best practices
• Mentor and develop reliability engineering teams
Required Skills
• Deep expertise in reliability engineering and asset management
• Strong analytical and data-driven decision-making skills
• Leadership and strategic planning
• Knowledge of predictive maintenance and Industry 4.0 technologies
• Root cause analysis and risk management
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 10–15+ years of experience
