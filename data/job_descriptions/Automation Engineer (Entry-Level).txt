Job Summary
An Automation Engineer is responsible for supporting the design, development, and
maintenance of automated systems used in manufacturing and industrial processes. The role
focuses on improving efficiency, reducing manual effort, and ensuring smooth automated
operations.
Key Responsibilities
• Assist in designing and implementing automation systems and processes
• Support PLC programming and control system development
• Work with sensors, actuators, and control panels
• Troubleshoot automation equipment and system issues
• Collaborate with production and maintenance teams
• Monitor system performance and suggest improvements
• Support installation, testing, and commissioning of automation systems
• Maintain documentation, wiring diagrams, and system reports
Required Skills
• Basic knowledge of automation and control systems
• Familiarity with PLCs (Siemens, Allen-Bradley)
• Understanding of sensors, actuators, and instrumentation
• Problem-solving and analytical skills
• Basic electrical knowledge (added advantage)
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Mechatronics / Electrical Engineering
• 0–2 years of experience
