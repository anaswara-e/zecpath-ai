Job Summary
An R&D Manager leads research initiatives, innovation strategies, and new technology
development. This role focuses on creating competitive advantages through advanced
engineering solutions and continuous improvement.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead R&D projects and innovation programs
• Define research strategies aligned with business goals
• Manage R&D teams and allocate resources
• Oversee prototype development and experimental testing
• Drive patent development and intellectual property initiatives
• Collaborate with design, production, and external partners
• Monitor emerging technologies and industry trends
• Prepare technical reports and presentations for leadership
Required Skills
• Strong research and analytical capabilities
• Leadership and innovation mindset

• Knowledge of advanced engineering tools and simulation
• Strategic thinking and problem-solving
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech (PhD preferred for advanced roles)
• 8–15 years of experience
