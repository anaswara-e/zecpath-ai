Job Summary
A Plant Manager is responsible for the overall management of a manufacturing facility,
ensuring smooth operations across production, maintenance, quality, and supply chain while
achieving business objectives.
Key Responsibilities

• Oversee complete plant operations and performance
• Develop and execute production and operational strategies
• Manage budgets, resources, and workforce
• Ensure compliance with safety, environmental, and regulatory standards
• Drive continuous improvement and operational excellence initiatives
• Coordinate across departments (production, QA, maintenance, logistics)
• Monitor KPIs such as productivity, cost, and efficiency
• Lead workforce development and training programs
• Handle vendor management and external stakeholders
Required Skills
• Strong leadership and organizational skills
• Strategic thinking and decision-making ability
• Deep knowledge of manufacturing operations
• Financial and budget management skills
• Expertise in Lean Manufacturing and process optimization
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (MBA preferred)
• 10–15+ years of experience
