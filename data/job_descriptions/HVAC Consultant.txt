Job Summary
An HVAC Consultant provides expert advice on designing, optimizing, and implementing
heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems for various projects.
Key Responsibilities
• Design HVAC systems for commercial/industrial buildings
• Perform load calculations and system sizing
• Recommend equipment and system configurations
• Ensure compliance with HVAC standards and energy efficiency norms
• Review and approve HVAC designs and installations

• Provide technical support and troubleshooting
• Optimize system performance and cost
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of HVAC design and standards
• Experience with tools (HAP, TRACE, AutoCAD)
• Energy efficiency and sustainability knowledge
• Consulting and client interaction skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 5–12+ years of experience
Work Model
• Consulting / Freelance / Project-based
