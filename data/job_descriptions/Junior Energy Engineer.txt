Job Summary
A Junior Energy Engineer focuses on improving energy efficiency, reducing energy
consumption, and supporting sustainability initiatives across industrial or commercial
facilities.
Key Responsibilities
• Assist in conducting energy audits and assessments
• Monitor energy consumption and analyze usage data
• Identify opportunities for energy savings and optimization
• Support implementation of energy-efficient technologies
• Prepare energy reports and performance dashboards
• Assist in renewable energy projects (solar, wind, etc.)
• Ensure compliance with energy regulations and standards
• Collaborate with engineering and operations teams
Required Skills
• Basic knowledge of energy systems and efficiency concepts
• Data analysis and reporting skills
• Understanding of renewable energy technologies
• Analytical and problem-solving ability
• Interest in sustainability and energy management
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Energy Engineering
• 0–2 years of experience
