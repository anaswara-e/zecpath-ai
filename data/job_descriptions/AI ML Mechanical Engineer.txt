Job Summary
An AI/ML Mechanical Engineer integrates machine learning and artificial intelligence into

mechanical systems to enable predictive analytics, automation, and intelligent decision-
making in engineering applications.

Key Responsibilities
• Develop AI/ML models for predictive maintenance, quality prediction, and
optimization
• Analyze large datasets from sensors, machines, and production systems
• Integrate AI solutions into mechanical and industrial processes
• Collaborate with data scientists and engineering teams
• Build and deploy machine learning pipelines
• Improve system efficiency using data-driven insights
• Work on computer vision and automation applications (if applicable)
Required Skills
• Machine learning (supervised, unsupervised learning)
• Programming (Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
• Data analysis and statistics
• Knowledge of mechanical systems and IoT data
• Problem-solving and analytical thinking
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering + AI/ML certification
• 2–6 years of experience
