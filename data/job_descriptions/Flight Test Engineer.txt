Job Summary
A Flight Test Engineer is responsible for planning, executing, and analyzing flight tests to
validate aircraft performance, safety, and compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan and conduct flight test programs
• Define test procedures and objectives
• Monitor and record flight test data
• Analyze performance, stability, and control characteristics
• Ensure safety during test operations
• Collaborate with pilots and engineering teams
• Prepare detailed test reports and documentation
• Support certification and regulatory approvals
Required Skills
• Knowledge of flight mechanics and aerodynamics
• Data analysis and instrumentation skills
• Strong communication and coordination
• Understanding of safety protocols
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Aerospace/Mechanical Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
