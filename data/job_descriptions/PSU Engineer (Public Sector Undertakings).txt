Job Summary

A PSU Engineer works in government-owned organizations handling large-scale industrial,
energy, and infrastructure projects, ensuring efficiency, safety, and compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage engineering operations in power plants, oil & gas, or manufacturing units
• Oversee installation, maintenance, and performance of equipment
• Ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards
• Monitor project execution and operational efficiency
• Coordinate with vendors, contractors, and internal teams
• Conduct inspections and performance analysis
• Prepare technical reports and documentation
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of mechanical systems and industrial operations
• Project management and coordination
• Problem-solving and analytical skills
• Understanding of government regulations and standards
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• Selection through GATE or PSU recruitment exams
