Job Summary
A Quality Manager is responsible for overseeing the organization’s quality assurance and
quality control functions, ensuring that products and processes meet internal standards,
customer expectations, and regulatory requirements.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop, implement, and maintain the Quality Management System (QMS)
• Ensure compliance with standards such as ISO 9001 and industry-specific regulations
• Lead quality assurance and quality control teams
• Monitor quality performance metrics (defects, rejection rates, customer complaints)
• Drive continuous improvement initiatives (Lean, Six Sigma, Kaizen)
• Conduct internal and external audits
• Manage non-conformance, CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Actions) processes
• Collaborate with production, design, and supply chain teams
• Handle customer quality issues and ensure timely resolution
• Train employees on quality standards and best practices
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of QMS and ISO standards
• Leadership and team management
• Root cause analysis and problem-solving
• Audit management and compliance expertise
• Data-driven decision-making
Qualifications

• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 8–12 years of experience
• Six Sigma Green/Black Belt (preferred)
