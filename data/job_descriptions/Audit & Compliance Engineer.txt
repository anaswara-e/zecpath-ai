Job Summary
An Audit & Compliance Engineer ensures that manufacturing processes and systems comply
with internal standards, regulatory requirements, and industry certifications. This role focuses
on audits, risk management, and regulatory adherence.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct internal and external quality audits (ISO, customer audits)
• Ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations
• Develop and maintain audit plans and schedules

• Identify non-conformities and ensure corrective actions (CAPA)
• Prepare audit reports and documentation
• Support certification processes (ISO 9001, ISO 14001, etc.)
• Collaborate with departments to improve compliance
• Train teams on compliance standards and audit readiness
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of ISO standards and compliance systems
• Audit planning and execution skills
• Documentation and reporting expertise
• Risk assessment and problem-solving
• Communication and training abilities
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 5–10 years of experience
• Lead Auditor Certification (preferred)
