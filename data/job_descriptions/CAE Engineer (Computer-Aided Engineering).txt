Job Summary
A CAE Engineer performs simulations and analyses to validate and optimize automotive
designs using tools like FEA and CFD.
Key Responsibilities
• Perform structural, thermal, and fluid simulations
• Develop and validate simulation models
• Analyze stress, fatigue, crash, and durability
• Optimize designs based on simulation results
• Collaborate with design teams for improvements
• Prepare simulation reports and documentation
Required Skills
• Expertise in CAE tools (ANSYS, Abaqus, HyperMesh)
• Strong knowledge of mechanics and materials
• Data analysis and interpretation
• Problem-solving skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
