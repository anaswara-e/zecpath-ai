Job Summary
An Equipment Engineer is responsible for managing, maintaining, and optimizing heavy
machinery and equipment used in construction and industrial projects.

Key Responsibilities
• Manage installation and operation of heavy equipment
• Ensure proper maintenance and performance of machinery
• Monitor equipment utilization and efficiency
• Troubleshoot mechanical issues and breakdowns
• Coordinate with vendors for equipment procurement and service
• Ensure compliance with safety and operational standards
• Maintain equipment records and documentation
• Optimize equipment usage and lifecycle management
Required Skills
• Knowledge of heavy equipment and machinery
• Maintenance and troubleshooting skills
• Understanding of construction operations
• Analytical and problem-solving abilities
• Vendor management and coordination
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–8 years of experience
