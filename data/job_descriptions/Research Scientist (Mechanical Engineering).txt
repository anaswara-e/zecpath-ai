Job Summary
A Research Scientist conducts advanced research in mechanical engineering fields such as
materials, energy systems, robotics, or fluid dynamics, contributing to innovation and
technological advancement.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct experimental and theoretical research
• Develop new technologies, products, or processes
• Publish research papers and patents
• Collaborate with academic, industrial, or government institutions
• Apply for research grants and funding
• Analyze data and prepare technical reports
• Supervise research assistants and students
Required Skills
• Strong analytical and research skills
• Expertise in a specialized domain (thermal, design, robotics, etc.)
• Data analysis and simulation tools
• Scientific writing and documentation
Qualifications
• M.Tech / Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering
• Research experience (academic or industrial)
