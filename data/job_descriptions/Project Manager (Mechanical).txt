Job Summary
A Project Manager is responsible for planning, executing, and delivering engineering or
construction projects within scope, budget, and timeline while ensuring quality and safety
compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan and manage end-to-end project lifecycle (initiation → execution → closure)
• Define project scope, objectives, timelines, and deliverables
• Develop detailed project schedules and resource plans
• Monitor project progress, costs, and risks
• Coordinate with design, procurement, and site teams
• Manage client relationships and stakeholder communication
• Ensure compliance with technical specifications, quality, and safety standards
• Resolve project issues and implement corrective actions
• Prepare project reports, documentation, and performance reviews

• Lead and mentor project teams
Required Skills
• Strong project management and leadership skills
• Knowledge of construction and mechanical systems
• Budgeting and cost control expertise
• Risk management and problem-solving
• Communication and stakeholder management
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (MBA/Project Management certification
preferred)
• 8–15 years of experience
