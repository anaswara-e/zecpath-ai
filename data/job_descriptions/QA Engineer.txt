Job Summary
A QA Engineer is responsible for developing, implementing, and maintaining quality
assurance systems to ensure products and processes meet required standards and regulatory
compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and maintain Quality Management Systems (QMS)
• Create and update quality procedures, SOPs, and work instructions
• Conduct internal audits and ensure ISO compliance (e.g., ISO 9001)
• Monitor process quality and implement improvement initiatives
• Perform root cause analysis (RCA) and corrective/preventive actions (CAPA)
• Collaborate with production, design, and quality teams
• Ensure compliance with customer and regulatory requirements
• Train employees on quality standards and procedures
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of QMS and ISO standards
• Root cause analysis tools (Fishbone, 5 Why, etc.)
• Documentation and audit handling
• Problem-solving and analytical thinking
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–6 years of experience
