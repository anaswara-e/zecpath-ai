Job Summary
A Trainee Engineer (Manufacturing) is an entry-level position designed for fresh graduates to
gain hands-on experience in plant operations, production processes, and industrial
engineering practices.
Key Responsibilities
• Assist in production planning and execution
• Learn and support machining, assembly, and fabrication processes

• Work under senior engineers to understand plant operations
• Participate in process improvement and quality initiatives
• Collect and analyze production data
• Support maintenance and troubleshooting activities
• Follow safety guidelines and company procedures
Required Skills
• Basic mechanical engineering knowledge
• Willingness to learn and adapt quickly
• Good observation and problem-solving ability
• Teamwork and communication skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• Freshers
