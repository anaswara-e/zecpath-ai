Job Summary
A Robotics Architect is a senior technical expert responsible for designing and defining the
architecture of complex robotic systems and automation solutions, often across multiple
projects or platforms.

Key Responsibilities
• Design high-level architecture for robotic and automation systems
• Define system requirements, frameworks, and standards
• Lead development of advanced robotic solutions (AI, vision systems, autonomous
systems)
• Integrate robotics with software, hardware, and control systems
• Provide technical leadership to robotics and automation teams
• Evaluate and implement new technologies in robotics and AI
• Ensure scalability, reliability, and performance of robotic systems
• Collaborate with stakeholders and business leaders for strategic planning
• Oversee system validation, testing, and deployment
Required Skills
• Deep expertise in robotics, automation, and control systems
• Strong programming skills (Python, C++, ROS)
• Knowledge of AI/ML, computer vision, and autonomous systems
• System architecture and design skills
• Leadership and innovation mindset
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical / Robotics / Mechatronics Engineering
• 10–15+ years of experience
