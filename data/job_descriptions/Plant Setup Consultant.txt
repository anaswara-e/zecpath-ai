Job Summary

A Plant Setup Consultant is responsible for planning, designing, and establishing new
manufacturing plants, ensuring efficient layout, equipment selection, and compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Plan plant layout and infrastructure design
• Select machinery, equipment, and technologies
• Oversee installation and commissioning of plant systems
• Ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards
• Optimize workflow, utilities, and production processes
• Coordinate with vendors, contractors, and stakeholders
• Manage project timelines and budgets
• Support initial production ramp-up
Required Skills
• Expertise in plant design and industrial engineering
• Knowledge of manufacturing systems and utilities
• Project management and vendor coordination
• Regulatory compliance understanding
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 10–20+ years of experience
Work Model
• Project-based consulting
