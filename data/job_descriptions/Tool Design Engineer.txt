Job Summary
A Tool Design Engineer is responsible for designing tools, jigs, fixtures, and dies used in
manufacturing processes such as machining, assembly, and mass production. The role
ensures efficiency, precision, and cost-effectiveness in production.
Key Responsibilities
• Design jigs, fixtures, dies, and cutting tools
• Develop 2D/3D CAD models and detailed drawings
• Optimize tool designs for manufacturing efficiency
• Collaborate with production and quality teams
• Analyze tool performance and implement improvements
• Ensure compliance with safety and quality standards
• Support tool trials and troubleshooting
Required Skills
• CAD tools (SolidWorks, CATIA, NX)
• Knowledge of machining and manufacturing processes
• Understanding of materials and heat treatment
• Problem-solving and analytical skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–8 years of experience
