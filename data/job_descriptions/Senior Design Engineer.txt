Job Summary
A Senior Design Engineer leads the design and development of complex mechanical systems,
ensuring high performance, reliability, and manufacturability. This role involves mentoring
junior engineers and driving design excellence across projects.
Key Responsibilities
• Lead end-to-end mechanical design projects
• Develop and review advanced CAD models and engineering drawings
• Perform complex engineering calculations and design validations
• Ensure compliance with standards, codes, and safety regulations
• Mentor and guide junior design engineers
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams (manufacturing, QA, R&D)
• Conduct design reviews and implement improvements
• Support prototype development and product testing
Required Skills
• Advanced CAD expertise (SolidWorks, CATIA, Creo)
• Strong knowledge of GD&T and design standards
• Problem-solving and analytical thinking
• Leadership and mentoring ability
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 6–10 years of experience
