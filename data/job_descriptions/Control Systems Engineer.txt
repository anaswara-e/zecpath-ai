Job Summary
A Control Systems Engineer designs, develops, and optimizes control systems used in
automation, robotics, and industrial processes to ensure stable and efficient system operation.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and develop control algorithms and systems
• Work with PLC, SCADA, and DCS systems
• Model and simulate control systems using tools like MATLAB/Simulink
• Tune controllers (PID, advanced control techniques)
• Integrate control systems with mechanical and electrical components
• Monitor system performance and implement improvements
• Troubleshoot and resolve control system issues
• Ensure system reliability and safety compliance
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of control theory and system dynamics
• Experience with PLC, SCADA, and industrial automation tools
• Programming skills (MATLAB, Python, C/C++)
• Analytical and problem-solving skills
• Understanding of sensors and instrumentation
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Electrical / Control Systems Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
