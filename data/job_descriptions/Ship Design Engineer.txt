Job Summary
A Ship Design Engineer is responsible for designing ship structures, systems, and layouts,
ensuring performance, safety, and compliance with maritime standards.
Key Responsibilities
• Design ship hulls, structures, and onboard systems
• Develop 3D models and technical drawings
• Perform structural and hydrodynamic analysis
• Ensure compliance with classification society rules (DNV, ABS, Lloyd’s)
• Collaborate with production and project teams
• Optimize designs for performance, cost, and manufacturability
• Prepare technical documentation and reports
• Support construction and testing phases
Required Skills
• Proficiency in CAD/CAE tools (AutoCAD, CATIA, Rhino, ANSYS)
• Knowledge of naval architecture and hydrodynamics
• Understanding of materials and marine standards
• Analytical and design skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Naval Architecture
• 3–7 years of experience
