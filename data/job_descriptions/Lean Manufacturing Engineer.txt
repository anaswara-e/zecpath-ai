Job Summary
A Lean Manufacturing Engineer focuses on eliminating waste, improving process efficiency,
and enhancing productivity using Lean principles and continuous improvement
methodologies.
Key Responsibilities
• Implement Lean tools (5S, Kaizen, Value Stream Mapping, Kanban)
• Identify waste and inefficiencies in production processes
• Improve workflow, reduce cycle time, and increase productivity
• Conduct process analysis and improvement projects
• Train employees on Lean practices and culture
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams for process improvements
• Monitor KPIs and track improvement results
• Support cost reduction and quality improvement initiatives
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of Lean Manufacturing principles
• Process analysis and problem-solving skills
• Data analysis and performance tracking
• Communication and training abilities
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Industrial Engineering
• 3–8 years of experience
• Lean certification (preferred)
