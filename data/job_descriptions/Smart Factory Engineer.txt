Job Summary
A Smart Factory Engineer designs and manages intelligent manufacturing systems that use
automation, IoT, and data analytics to create highly efficient and connected production
environments.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and implement smart factory systems
• Integrate automation, robotics, and IoT technologies
• Monitor and analyze production data in real-time
• Improve production efficiency and reduce downtime
• Collaborate with IT and operations teams
• Implement digital dashboards and control systems
• Drive continuous improvement initiatives
Required Skills
• Knowledge of IoT, automation, and data analytics
• Experience with manufacturing systems and MES
• Programming and system integration skills
• Problem-solving and innovation mindset
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Mechatronics Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
