Job Summary
A Sustainability Engineer focuses on reducing environmental impact by improving energy
efficiency, minimizing waste, and implementing sustainable engineering practices.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop sustainability strategies and initiatives
• Conduct environmental impact assessments
• Optimize energy and resource usage
• Implement waste reduction and recycling programs
• Ensure compliance with environmental regulations
• Support green building and renewable energy projects
• Prepare sustainability reports and metrics
Required Skills
• Knowledge of sustainability and environmental engineering
• Energy management and efficiency techniques
• Data analysis and reporting
• Regulatory compliance understanding
• Problem-solving and strategic thinking
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Environmental Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
