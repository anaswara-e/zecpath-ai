Job Summary
An Energy Manager is responsible for planning, monitoring, and optimizing energy
consumption across industrial or commercial facilities. The role focuses on improving energy
efficiency, reducing operational costs, and ensuring compliance with energy regulations and
sustainability goals.
Key Responsibilities
• Develop and implement energy management strategies and policies
• Monitor energy consumption and analyze usage patterns
• Conduct energy audits and identify savings opportunities
• Implement energy efficiency and cost-reduction initiatives
• Manage renewable energy projects (solar, wind, etc.)
• Ensure compliance with energy regulations and standards
• Track and report energy KPIs and sustainability metrics
• Collaborate with operations, maintenance, and finance teams
• Evaluate new technologies for energy optimization
• Lead awareness and training programs on energy efficiency
Required Skills
• Strong knowledge of energy systems and efficiency techniques
• Experience with energy auditing and analysis tools
• Data analysis and reporting skills
• Understanding of renewable energy technologies
• Project management and leadership skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical / Energy Engineering

• 8–12 years of experience
• Certified Energy Manager (CEM) – preferred
