Job Summary
A Process Engineer focuses on designing, implementing, and continuously improving
manufacturing processes to ensure efficiency, consistency, and quality.

Key Responsibilities
• Design and develop manufacturing processes and workflows
• Monitor and optimize process performance
• Identify bottlenecks and implement corrective actions
• Conduct root cause analysis and process improvements
• Develop standard operating procedures (SOPs)
• Ensure process compliance with quality and safety standards
• Work closely with production and quality teams
• Support cost reduction and waste minimization initiatives
Required Skills
• Strong analytical and process optimization skills
• Knowledge of Lean, Six Sigma, and Kaizen methodologies
• Understanding of industrial processes and equipment
• Data-driven decision-making ability
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical or Industrial Engineering
• 3–7 years experience
