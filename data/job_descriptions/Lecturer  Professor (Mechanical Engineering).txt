Job Summary
A Lecturer/Professor is responsible for teaching mechanical engineering subjects, mentoring
students, and contributing to academic research and institutional development.
Key Responsibilities
• Teach undergraduate and postgraduate courses (Thermodynamics, Mechanics,
Manufacturing, etc.)
• Prepare lecture materials, assignments, and examinations
• Guide student projects, internships, and research work
• Conduct academic research and publish papers in journals
• Participate in curriculum development and academic planning
• Attend conferences, workshops, and faculty development programs
• Mentor students for career growth and higher studies
• Contribute to institutional administration and committees
Required Skills
• Strong subject knowledge in mechanical engineering
• Teaching and presentation skills
• Research and academic writing ability
• Communication and mentoring skills
Qualifications

• M.E./M.Tech (Lecturer)
• Ph.D. (Professor / Associate Professor preferred)
• NET/SET qualification (for government colleges in India)
