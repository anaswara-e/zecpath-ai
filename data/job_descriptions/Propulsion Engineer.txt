Job Summary
A Propulsion Engineer designs and develops propulsion systems such as jet engines, rocket
engines, and fuel systems for aircraft and spacecraft.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and analyze propulsion systems and components
• Perform thermodynamic and fluid flow analysis
• Optimize engine performance and fuel efficiency
• Conduct testing and validation of propulsion systems
• Ensure compliance with aerospace standards
• Collaborate with design and testing teams
• Troubleshoot and improve system performance
Required Skills

• Strong knowledge of thermodynamics and fluid mechanics
• Experience with simulation tools (CFD, ANSYS Fluent)
• Understanding of combustion and fuel systems
• Analytical and problem-solving skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech or M.Tech in Mechanical/Aerospace Engineering
• 3–7 years of experience
