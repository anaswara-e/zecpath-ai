Job Summary
A Mechanical Design Engineer specializes in designing mechanical systems such as
machines, equipment, and assemblies, ensuring performance, durability, and efficiency.
Key Responsibilities
• Design mechanical systems and assemblies
• Perform stress, thermal, and motion analysis
• Select appropriate materials and components
• Create detailed CAD models and engineering drawings
• Collaborate with manufacturing for design feasibility
• Conduct failure analysis and design improvements
• Ensure compliance with design standards and codes
Required Skills
• Advanced CAD tools (SolidWorks, Creo, CATIA)
• Knowledge of FEA basics
• Strong understanding of mechanics and materials
• Problem-solving and analytical skills
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
• 3–6 years experience
