Job Summary
A Graduate Mechanical Engineer is an entry-level role designed for fresh engineering
graduates to gain hands-on experience in design, production, quality, or maintenance. The
role focuses on learning core engineering practices, supporting senior engineers, and
contributing to projects.
Key Responsibilities
• Assist in mechanical design, analysis, and development tasks
• Support production and manufacturing processes
• Work on basic CAD modeling and engineering drawings
• Conduct data collection, testing, and documentation
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams (design, QA, production)
• Participate in troubleshooting and problem-solving activities
• Follow company engineering standards and safety protocols
Required Skills
• Basic understanding of mechanical engineering principles
• Knowledge of CAD tools (AutoCAD / SolidWorks preferred)
• Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
• Good communication and teamwork ability
• Willingness to learn and adapt
Qualifications
• B.E./B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering or related field
• Freshers or 0–1 year experience
